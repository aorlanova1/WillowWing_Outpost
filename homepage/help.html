HOME WILLOW-WING OUTPOST : HELP


Saving
I can't play/my game won't save Willow-Wing Outpost relies on local storage for saving. If you are blocking all cookies on your browser, some browsers won't let you play. Try to allow cookies in your browser's privacy settings.
How can I keep my save? Your horses and items will be kept as long as local storage/cache is not cleared. This is usually done where you clear browser history, so if you want to keep your save file, be careful when selecting options in clearing cookies/local storage/history.
Browser data will not be stored across different browsers. A save file on Safari won't be available on Chrome.
If you really want to keep all data, always, but want to disalow cookies normally, you can use a seperate browser for just this game in which you don't clear your cache.
Horses
How do I catch a wild horse ~Walk over to a tile a wild horse is on. This will trigger an event in the event log, click the catch button in the event to start the minigame.
~Once the minigame starts, press 'spacebar' to send a lasso. If your lasso catches the horse, you will have caught a wild! You have 5 lasso throws per game.
Owned horse information ~Open the horse menu on the top of the game screen by pressing the button 'HORSES'.
~ Here you can toggle whether or not horses are in the stable with 'Toggle Display,' ride or dismount with, 'Toggle Ride,' and see horse information in 'info'
